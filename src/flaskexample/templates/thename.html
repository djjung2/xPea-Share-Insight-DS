Why xPea Share?


You might be wondering why I named my product xPea Share. There are no peas involved in my product (except for me saying "Peas and thank you" for using it). Why would I give it such a name?

xPea Share is built upon 2.5 million people reviewing jobs on Glassdoor and occupational experts judging what skills are necessary for almost a thousand jobs. People shared their experiences of what certain jobs are like, with the hope of helping you find your job. That's why I wanted to incorpate "sharing experiences" in the name of my system.

For why I chose xPea Share in particular, this goes back to my favorite video game series as a child: Pokemon. This Japanese video game series has been played by tens of millions globally and playing it was one of my favorite childhood memories. The premise is that you, a Pokemon trainer, capture small animals called Pokemon and make them stronger over time by fighting other Pokemon.

One item in this game series is called Exp. Share. The item may have been intended to be read as "Experience Share", but I always read it as "xPea Share". If you give this item to one of your Pokemon, then whenever one of your Pokemon beats another Pokemon, the Pokemon holding Exp. Share will also gain experience from the battle. It is extremely helpful for training less experienced Pokemon become stronger so that with enough teamwork, the less experienced will eventually become powerful enough in order to contribute to the team.

And that's what I think xPea Share is: Millions of people coming together to share their experiences in order to help you gain the experience you need.
