About me

Derek Jung


Hello! I recently finished my Ph.D. in Mathematics at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, where I studied analysis and geometry. For my research, I was essentially interested in the best way to steer a car pulling multiple trailers while minimizing gas usage and, you know, obeying laws of physics.

I completed this project xPea in 3 weeks as a part of the Fall 2018 session of Insight Data Science in New York City. It was an exciting project to create and develop from end-to-end. I would be happy to talk with you more about my project or anything else. I live in Long Island City and am currently looking for a job in the New York City area. Feel free to reach out to me at drkjjung@gmail.com or check out my website at sites.google.com/site/derekjjung!
